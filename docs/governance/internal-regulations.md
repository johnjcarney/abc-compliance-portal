# Internal Regulations on Personal Data Protection

**Effective Date:** September 2024  
**Legal Basis:** Vietnam Decree No. 13/2023/ND-CP

---

## 1. Purpose
This document serves as the supreme internal regulation regarding the processing of personal data at **ABC International School**. It defines our legal obligations as a **Data Controller** and **Data Processor**.

## 2. Roles & Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Data Controller** | The School Board. Determines *why* and *how* data is used. |
| **Data Protection Officer (DPO)** | Responsible for monitoring compliance and liaising with the Ministry of Public Security. |
| **Staff Member** | Any employee handling student or parent data. |

## 3. Data Subject Rights
Under Article 9 of Decree 13, all students, parents, and staff have the right to:
1.  **Know** how their data is being processed.
2.  **Consent** or withdraw consent at any time.
3.  **Request deletion** of their data (unless required by law).

!!! warning "Non-Compliance"
    Failure to adhere to these regulations may result in disciplinary action and personal liability under Vietnamese law.
