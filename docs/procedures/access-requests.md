# Handling Data Subject Requests (SAR)

## Overview
Parents and staff can request to see, correct, or delete their data. We have **72 hours** to acknowledge these requests.

## The Procedure

### Step 1: Verification
!!! abstract "Check Identity"
    Ensure the person requesting the data is actually who they say they are.
    * **In Person:** Check ID Card.
    * **Email:** Verify the email address matches our records. call to confirm if unsure.

### Step 2: Locate Data
Check the following systems:
* School Management System (SIS)
* Admissions CRM
* Clinic Records

### Step 3: Response
Send the data via the **Secure Transfer Tool**. Do not attach raw Excel files to standard emails.
